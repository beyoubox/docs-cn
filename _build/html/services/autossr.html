AutoSSR: 免费 ShadowsocksR¶

  • Github: https://github.com/stoken100g/AutoSSR

Note

You can find several ShadowsocksR services here, we will check all the servers once per hour, and THEY ARE FREE!!

Subscribe by URL¶

Choose one or more URL below of the 3 levels, and add to your SSR client:

ONLINE

All online servers, available more than 1 hour.

https://git.io/autossr_online
https://raw.githubusercontent.com/stoken100g/AutoSSR/master/online
STABLE

Available more than 6 hours, seems they are more stable.

https://git.io/autossr_stable
https://raw.githubusercontent.com/stoken100g/AutoSSR/master/stable
RECENT

Recent servers, active in 12 hours.

https://git.io/autossr_recent
https://raw.githubusercontent.com/stoken100g/AutoSSR/master/recent
