How to read the Stoken Public-Sale Contract?¶

The screenshots and data in this page are from the Ethereum kovan test network and they are for demonstration purposes only.

For real-time business data reading please visit: 在Etherscan.io中查看合约

Status and data¶

Main status - 1. status¶

Let’s focus on the values:

auditEtherPrice       |uint256 :  170000000
stage                 |uint16 :   81
season                |uint16 :   1
stokenUsdPrice         |uint256 :  1810
currentTopSalesRatio  |uint256 :  15067500
txs                   |uint256 :  4
stokenTxs              |uint256 :  82
stokenBonusTxs         |uint256 :  3
stokenWhitelistTxs     |uint256 :  4
stokenIssued           |uint256 :  8193922651933
stokenBonus            |uint256 :  810000000000
stokenWhitelist        |uint256 :  9003922651933
auditEtherPrice

Current Audit ETH Price, in USD, with 6 decimals.

In this case, 170000000 means you can use 1 ETH as $ 170.000000 USD, for now.

stage

Stage number.

81 means: It’s in Stage #81.

season

Season number.

1 means: It’s in Season #1.

stokenUsdPrice

Current Stoken price, in USD, with 6 decimals.

1810 means: Current Stoken price is $ 0.001810 USD .

currentTopSalesRatio

Current Top-Sales rewards ratio, with 8 decimals.

15067500 is 0.15067500, means 15.067500% of the last transaction ETH amount will be stored in the Top-Sales Rewards Pool. It is calculated by Stage number, if the Public-Sale goes into next stage, ratio increases.

txs

Transactions amount in this Stoken Public-Sale.

4 means: There are 4 transactions since the Stoken Public-Sale started.

stokenTxs

Stoken transactions amount.

82 means: There are 82 transactions of Stokens finished already.

stokenBonusTxs

Stoken bonus transactions amount.

3 means: There are 3 transaction of Stoken bonus finished already.

stokenWhitelistTxs

Stoken whitelist transactions amount.

4 means: There are 4 transactions of Stoken whitelist finished already.

stokenIssued

Stoken issued amount transferred already, with 6 decimals.

8193922651933 is 8,193,922.651933 Stokens.

stokenBonus

Stoken bonus amount transferred already.

810000000000 is 810,000.000000 Stokens.

stokenWhitelist

Stoken whitelist amount transferred already.

9003922651933 is 9,003,922.651933 Stokens.

Sum of the main data - 6. sum¶

Let’s focus on the values:

weiSold              |uint256 :  67705882352941176472
weiReferralRewarded  |uint256 :  19708588235294117625
weiTopSales          |uint256 :  10180962911764705838
weiTeam              |uint256 :  33820000000000000000
weiPending           |uint256 :  3988470588235294117
weiPendingTransfered |uint256 :  0
weiPendingRemain     |uint256 :  3988470588235294117

All the values is in wei (minimum unit of ETH, 1 wei = 0.000000000000000001 ETH), there are 18 decimals.

weiSold

Amount of Public-Sales revenue.

67705882352941176472 is 67.705882352941176472 ETH.

weiReferralRewarded

Amount of Referral-Reward already sent.

19708588235294117625 is 19.708588235294117625 ETH.

weiTopSales

Amount of Top-Sales Reward already counted.

10180962911764705838 is 10.180962911764705838 ETH.

weiTeam

Beyou.Network project team received.

33820000000000000000 is 33.82 ETH.

weiPending

Unallocated ETH rewards pending pool.

3988470588235294117 is 3.988470588235294117 ETH.

weiPendingTransfered

Unallocated ETH rewards already transferred.

0 is 0 ETH.

weiPendingRemain

Unallocated ETH rewards pending pool remain. Should be always = weiPending - weiPendingTransfered.

3988470588235294117 is 3.988470588235294117 ETH.

Status of a specific stage - 3. stageStatus¶

Enter a specific stage number, and press Query, you will get a response:

For example, Stage #81:

stokenUsdPrice     uint256 :  1810
stokenCap          uint256 :  100000000000
stokenOnSale       uint256 :  6077348067
stokenSold         uint256 :  93922651933
usdCap            uint256 :  181000000
usdOnSale         uint256 :  11000000
usdSold           uint256 :  170000000
weiTopSalesRatio  uint256 :  15067500
stokenUsdPrice

Stoken price of the stage, in USD, with 6 decimals.

1810 is $ 0.001810 USD.

stokenCap

Stoken amount cap of the stage, with 6 decimals.

100000000000 is 100,000.000000 Stokens.

stokenOnSale

Stoken on-sale of the stage, with 6 decimals.

6077348067 is 6,077.348067 Stokens.

stokenSold

Stoken sold of the stage, with 6 decimals.

93922651933 is 93,922.651933 Stokens.

usdCap

USD amount cap of the stage, with 6 decimals.

181000000 is $ 181.000000 USD.

usdOnSale

USD on-sale of the stage, with 6 decimals.

11000000 is $ 11.000000 USD.

usdSold

USD sold of the stage, with 6 decimals.

170000000 is $ 170.000000 USD.

weiTopSalesRatio

Top-Sales rewards ratio of the stage, with 8 decimals.

15067500 is 0.15067500, means 15.067500%.

Top-Sales rewards of a specific season - 11. seasonTopSalesRewards¶

Enter a specific season number, and press Query, you will get a response:

For example, Season #1:

weiSold                 uint256 :  67705882352941176432
weiTopSales             uint256 :  10180962911764705838
weiTopSalesTransfered   uint256 :  0
weiTopSalesRemain       uint256 :  10180962911764705838

All the values is in wei (minimum unit of ETH, 1 wei = 0.000000000000000001 ETH), there are 18 decimals.

weiSold

Amount of the sales revenue, in the season.

67705882352941176432 is 67.705882352941176432 ETH.

weiTopSales

Top-Sales Reward already counted, in the season.

10180962911764705838 is 10.180962911764705838 ETH.

weiTopSalesTransfered

Top-Sales Reward already transferred, in the season.

0 is 0 ETH.

weiTopSalesRemain

Top-Sales Reward remain, in the season.

10180962911764705838 is 10.180962911764705838 ETH.

Query data which may relate to you¶

Query an account 13. accountQuery¶

Enter a specific ETH wallet address, and press Query, you will get a response:

For example, query an address of 0x02eEe50… in the entire stoken public-sale:

stokenIssued          uint256 :  913930751207
stokenBonus           uint256 :  91393075120
stokenWhitelisted     uint256 :  1005323826327
weiPurchased         uint256 :  10000000000000000000
weiReferralRewarded  uint256 :  21296117647058823526
stokenIssued

Stoken issued amount, with 6 decimals.

913930751207 is 913,930.751207 Stokens.

stokenBonus

Stoken bonus amount, with 6 decimals.

91393075120 is 91,393.075120 Stokens.

stokenWhitelisted

Stoken whitelist amount, with 6 decimals.

1005323826327 is 1,005,323.826327 Stokens.

weiPurchased

Amount of ETH purchased, with 18 decimals.

10000000000000000000 is 10 ETH.

weiReferralRewarded

Amount of Referral-Reward received, with 18 decimals.

21296117647058823526 is 21.296117647058823526 ETH.
